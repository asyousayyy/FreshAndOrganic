About Fresh & Organic

Our Mission

At Fresh & Organic, our mission is to provide our customers with the freshest and highest quality organic produce. We believe in the power of natural, healthy food and are committed to delivering it right to your doorstep. Our platform connects you with locally sourced fruits, vegetables, and other organic products, ensuring you get the best without compromising on quality.

Our Services

We offer a seamless e-commerce experience designed to cater to your fresh produce needs:

  • Secure Login/Logout: Our system includes robust login/logout features with SHA-256 encryption for your security, along with a forgotten password functionality and CSRF protection.
  • Personalized Shopping Cart: Manage up to 10 units per product with ease and keep track of your order history through personalized profiles.
  • Seller Support: Verified users can become sellers, managing their products with full CRUD operations on their listings and inventory.
  • Product Search & Payment: Quickly find what you need with our product search feature, and enjoy secure payments with an integrated gateway, including an option for cash on delivery with a nominal fee of 1$.
  • Customer Service: Our dedicated customer service team is always available to assist with inquiries and support.
